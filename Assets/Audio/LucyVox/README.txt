Story Line Beats

Tutorial - 

1. LetsGo - Plays after Han says "Those ghosts aren't gonna exorcise themselves!"

2. Huh - When Han reveals the ghost around the corner

3. Hmmm... - When the tutorial popup comes up 


Bossfight Arena - 

1. Han! - when Lucy sees Han across the arena, he's been captured!

2. Hold On! - When Lucy approaches the cage

3. Bring it! - When Lucy starts the Gertrude fight


Hmmph, Hmm, and Huh are generic and be used during any sort of inner monologue
